Frequently Asked Questions

Find the information you need to get started with ParentLink and maximize your experience.

How do I create an account on ParentLink?

To create an account, navigate to the registration page, fill out the required information, and submit the form. You will receive an email confirmation to activate your account.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, head over to the Contact Us section, fill in your information and a query, and we’ll send you the password associated to your account.

How does ParentLink integrate with Blackboard?

ParentLink uses Blackboard's API to retrieve real-time data, including grades, attendance, and course progress. This integration allows parents to monitor their child’s academic performance effectively.

Can I monitor multiple students with one account?

Yes, you can monitor multiple students by linking your ParentLink account with each child’s Blackboard profile. This allows you to view each student’s academic details in one place.

What kind of notifications will I receive?

ParentLink sends notifications to the parents' email regarding the Blackboard Ultra account credentials.

Is my data secure on ParentLink?

Yes, data security is a top priority. ParentLink uses industry-standard encryption and secure API connections to protect your personal and academic information.

How do I contact support for additional help?

If you need assistance, visit our Contact Us section to submit a support request. Our team will respond as quickly as possible to address your concerns.
